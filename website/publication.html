SotA Lens paper, preprint, and software

The repository includes the public software release, the browser-based explorer, a concise JOSS software paper, and a longer arXiv-style methodology preprint.

JOSS manuscript

The JOSS manuscript is included under paper/paper.md, with references in paper/paper.bib. It is the compact software paper for journal submission.

Open JOSS paper directory

arXiv preprint

The longer methodology paper is included under arxiv/main.tex. It expands the comparison with PRISMA, PRISMA-ScR, systematic mapping, and bibliometric mapping, and documents the DPM/SAR proof of concept.

Open arXiv source directory

Case-study demo

The website includes the Dynamic Projection-Mapping / Spatial Augmented Reality demonstration corpus used in the paper and preprint.
