SotA Lens software, preprint, and archival status

SotA Lens is released as open-source research software and archived through Zenodo. The arXiv preprint is pending endorsement/submission, and the JOSS manuscript is available for transparency and possible future resubmission after a longer public open-development period.

Current status

  • Software DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.19860899.
  • Software release: latest GitHub release, archived through Zenodo.
  • arXiv preprint: pending endorsement/submission. A rendered PDF is available below while the arXiv identifier is pending.
  • JOSS: manuscript available, but not currently under review. The first submission was procedurally rejected because JOSS requires a longer public open-development history for projects that were privately developed before public release.

arXiv preprint source

The longer methodology paper is included under arxiv/main.tex. It expands the comparison with PRISMA, PRISMA-ScR, systematic mapping, and bibliometric mapping, and documents the DPM/SAR proof of concept.
